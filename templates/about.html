About Namma Metro

Bangalore's Pride - Namma Metro

Namma Metro is Bengaluru's rapid transit system—safe, eco-friendly, and efficient. Connecting the vibrant city of Bangalore, our metro system represents innovation, sustainability, and progress. With cutting-edge technology and world-class infrastructure, we provide millions of commuters with a reliable, comfortable, and environmentally conscious mode of transportation.


Since our inauguration in 2011, we have continuously expanded our network to serve more areas of the city, reducing traffic congestion and promoting sustainable urban mobility. Our commitment extends beyond transportation - we're building a greener, more connected Bangalore for future generations.
